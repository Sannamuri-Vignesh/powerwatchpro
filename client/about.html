🔍 About PowerWatch

PowerWatch is a community-driven web application built to help citizens monitor, report, and resolve electricity issues in their local areas. Our mission is to bring transparency and timely resolution to power-related problems through modern, accessible technology.

💡 Our Vision

To empower every citizen with a platform that enables quick action and awareness regarding electricity issues — ensuring safer, smarter, and more connected communities.

🔧 How It Works

Users can submit reports for power outages, voltage fluctuations, and other electrical problems. Admins review these reports, track patterns, and respond accordingly.

If you're passionate about community improvement or technology for good, we welcome your contribution — as a user, volunteer, or developer!
